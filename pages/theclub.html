About us

So who are we?

Saint-Petersburg Working Retriever Club was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate retriever owners who discovered their keen interest in the retriever sport.

They quickly realized, however, that Russian retriever scene is much less developed than the European or let alone British one. The only existing club for working retriever owners was located in Moscow, rendering any possibilities of participating in training sessions or competitions for people from Saint-Petersburg very unlikely, if not completely impossible

And this is how Saint-Petersburg Working Retriever Club came about. Started as small group of five like-minded individuals who gathered together to train in the fields, the club grew in size to just over a few dozen members in the next years, began organizing its own events and even small competitions as well as collaborating with other clubs, including those from the Baltic and Finland.

Today the club offers a range of activities for dog owners of all levels - from simple obedience and dummy training sessions for the very beginners to drives, field trials and working tests in Open Class for experienced handlers and dogs.
